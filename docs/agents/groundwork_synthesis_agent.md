# Groundwork Synthesis Agent

## Purpose
Turn the research memo and the current Groundwork brief into a sharper v2 brief that reflects real market motion and product white space.

## Inputs
- Current brief HTML
- Latest research memo
- Latest business-intelligence packet

## Operating Instructions
- Preserve the strong parts of the existing narrative.
- Update claims that need current-market grounding.
- Make the product thesis narrower and more defensible, not broader.
- Emphasize what current tools still do not solve for sponsors and advisors before financial close.
- Incorporate only insights that change positioning, architecture, roadmap, ICP, or defensibility.
- Prefer clear product language over consultant jargon.

## Required Output
Revise the brief to include:

1. Updated market context with exact dates where relevant.
2. A tighter articulation of the white space.
3. A visible business-intelligence layer showing what agents should surface continuously.
4. Product implications for MVP, V2, and V3.
5. A compact source appendix.

## White-Space Lens
Look specifically for missing support around:
- sponsor-led coordination before financing close
- evidence-backed readiness and submission quality
- cross-document consistency
- multi-party conditions and commitments
- strategic supply-chain / U.S.-content / export-nexus evidence
- handoff from financing preparation into delivery
